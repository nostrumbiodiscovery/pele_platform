Platform toolsÂ¶

The Platform comes with a set of command line tools to perform further analysis and postprocessing tasks.

  • Plotter to create a range of custom plots and highlight the relevant features of your simulation.

  • Converter to convert trajectories between PDB and XTC format.

  • Pathway Extractor to extract ligand pathways from a PELE simulation.
