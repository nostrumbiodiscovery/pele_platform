=============
PELE Platform
=============

.. image:: img/pele_logo.png
    :width: 30%
    :align: center

PELE Platform is a suite of packages that aims to find novel solutions to drug discovery problems in a matter
of hours. Most of its tools revolve around PELE, our proprietary software to explore the potential
energy surface of biochemical systems.
